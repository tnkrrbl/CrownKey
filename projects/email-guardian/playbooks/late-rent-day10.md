# Playbook: Late Rent — Day 10
## CrownKey Email Guardian — Template v1.0

**Situation:** Tenant's rent payment has not been received as of 10 days past the due date. A Day 3 reminder was already sent (or skipped per policy). This communication is more formal, references the late fee that has been assessed, and clearly states the consequence of continued non-payment. This is still an email communication — it is not a formal legal notice.

**Recipient Type:** Tenant

**Risk Level:** Medium — this email references consequences and must not cross into formal legal notice territory unless that is the intent.

**Crown Key Policy (Attorney Reviewed):**
- This email is a communication — it is NOT a formal legal notice. If a formal 3-Day Notice to Pay or Quit is required, it is served separately via first-class mail and/or personal delivery (post and mail). Email alone does not constitute valid legal service under Crown Key's leases.
- All Crown Key properties are exempt from AB 1482 (California Tenant Protection Act).
- Attorney review complete. This template is approved for use.

**Usage Note:** Send this email as a courtesy communication. Serve the formal 3-Day Notice simultaneously or immediately after via post and mail.

---

## Email Template

**Subject:** Important — [Month] Rent Past Due at [Property Address]

---

Hi [Tenant First Name],

This is a follow-up regarding your [Month] rent payment at [Property Address]. As of today, [Current Date], we have not received your payment of $[Rent Amount], which was due on [Due Date].

Per your lease agreement, a late fee of $[Late Fee Amount] was assessed on [Late Fee Assessment Date]. Your total balance due is currently $[Total Amount Due].

We want to resolve this as quickly as possible. Please submit your full balance immediately through your AppFolio resident portal:

[Portal Link or Instructions]

If we do not receive your payment by [Final Deadline Date], we will be required to proceed with a formal Notice to Pay or Quit as required by California law, which begins the legal process for your tenancy.

If you are experiencing a financial hardship or need to discuss your situation, please contact us directly at [Phone Number] or reply to this email. We want to hear from you — reaching out is always better than going silent.

[Manager First Name]
Crown Key Realty
[Phone Number]
[Email Address]

## Usage Notes

- This template assumes the Day 3 reminder has already been sent. If it was not, note that in the file and consult with Susan before sending.
- Do not customize the late fee amount or waive fees informally via email. Fee waivers require written documentation separate from this template.
- If the tenant has communicated a hardship or payment plan, this template is NOT appropriate — use a negotiated response instead.
- This email should be sent simultaneously with or shortly after any formal paper notice if formal notice is required. Consult Mason on whether formal notice is also required at Day 10 under Crown Key's lease terms.
- Send this email at the same time to all tenants with outstanding balances on Day 10 — no variation by tenant.
