# Playbook: Late Rent — Day 3
## CrownKey Email Guardian — Template v1.0

**Situation:** Tenant's rent payment has not been received as of 3 days past the due date. This is a first-contact reminder. The tone is friendly and assumes good faith — this may be an oversight, a technical issue with the portal, or a payment that is in transit.

**Recipient Type:** Tenant

**Risk Level:** Low — this is a courtesy reminder, not a legal notice.

**What Mason Needs to Check:**
- Confirm the late fee grace period referenced is consistent with California law and the current lease form. California law does not require a grace period, but if the lease grants one, it must be honored.
- Confirm it is appropriate to send this communication by email vs. formal written notice under the lease.
- Confirm the email does not constitute a "notice to pay or quit" (it should not — this is a friendly reminder only).
- Confirm no language could be read as waiving Crown Key's right to collect late fees.

---

## Email Template

**Subject:** Quick Check-In — [Month] Rent at [Property Address]

---

Hi [Tenant First Name],

I wanted to reach out quickly because our records show your [Month] rent payment of $[Amount] has not yet been received. It was due on [Date].

If you've already submitted your payment, please disregard this message — processing can sometimes take a day or two and we may simply not have it posted yet.

If you haven't had a chance to send it yet, please go ahead and submit through your AppFolio resident portal as soon as possible.

[Portal Link or Instructions]

If you're experiencing any difficulty with your payment, please give us a call or send us a reply and we'd be happy to help figure out next steps.

Thank you — we appreciate you staying in touch.

[Manager First Name]
Crown Key Realty
[Phone Number]
[Email Address]

---

## Placeholder Reference

| Placeholder | What to Fill In |
|---|---|
| [Tenant First Name] | Tenant's first name |
| [Month] | The rent month (e.g., "July") |
| [Amount] | The total amount due |
| [Date] | The original due date (e.g., "July 1") |
| [Portal Link or Instructions] | AppFolio portal URL or instructions for how to pay |
| [Manager First Name] | Sending manager's first name |
| [Phone Number] | Crown Key's main office or manager's direct line |
| [Email Address] | Crown Key's reply-to email |

---

## Usage Notes

- Send this email on Day 3 only if no payment has posted and no communication has been received from the tenant.
- Do not send this email if the tenant has already contacted the office about the payment — switch to a conversation-based response instead.
- Do not reference late fees in this email. This is a friendly reminder. Late fee language belongs in the Day 10 template.
- If the tenant responds saying they are experiencing hardship, escalate to Susan / office manager for a conversation — do not handle hardship situations via email alone.
