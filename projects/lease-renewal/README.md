# Lease Renewal Tracker — Crown Key Realty

## How to use this tool

1. **Open the file** — double-click `lease-renewal.html` to open it in your browser (Chrome or Safari). No setup needed.

2. **Add your tenants** — click the "Tenant List" tab, then "Add Tenant." Enter the tenant's name, unit number, and lease end date, then click Save.

3. **Check the Dashboard** — the Dashboard tab shows every lease expiring in the next 60 days, sorted by soonest first. Red rows need attention within 30 days; yellow rows have 31–60 days remaining.

4. **Send renewal emails** — click "60-Day Email" or "30-Day Email" next to any tenant. A ready-to-send email will appear. Click "Copy to Clipboard," then paste it into Gmail.

5. **Edit or remove tenants** — go to the Tenant List tab and use the Edit or Delete buttons on any row.

6. **Your data is saved automatically** — everything is stored in your browser. It will still be there the next time you open the file, as long as you use the same browser on the same computer.

> Note: If you clear your browser history or switch to a different browser, your tenant list will not carry over.
